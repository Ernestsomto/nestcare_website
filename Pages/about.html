Frequently Asked Questions About Our Cleaning Services

What areas of the UK do you serve? We provide professional cleaning services across the entire United Kingdom including London, Manchester, Birmingham, Glasgow, Liverpool, Bristol, Leeds, Edinburgh, Cardiff and all major cities and towns. We're constantly expanding our coverage areas.

Are your cleaners insured and vetted? Yes, all our cleaning professionals are fully insured with public liability insurance up to £5 million. They undergo rigorous background checks including DBS checks, reference verification, and comprehensive training before joining our team.

What cleaning products do you use? We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for children, pets, and the environment. Our products effectively eliminate germs and allergens without harsh chemicals. We also offer specialized products for specific cleaning needs upon request.

How do I book a cleaning service? You can book our services easily through our website contact form, by phone, or via WhatsApp. Simply provide your contact details, preferred service type, and scheduling preferences. Our team will confirm your booking and provide a free, no-obligation quote.

Our Story

NestCare Cleaning Services was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide exceptional cleaning services that transform spaces and bring peace of mind to homes and businesses across the United Kingdom. What started as a small startup has grown into one of the most trusted cleaning companies in the UK, serving thousands of satisfied customers nationwide.

Our Mission

At NestCare, we believe that a clean environment is fundamental to health, productivity, and happiness. Our mission is to deliver superior cleaning results that exceed expectations while using eco-friendly products and sustainable practices. We're committed to making every space we touch cleaner, healthier, and more welcoming.
